Project 2

For this project, I chose the theme of a tailgate party before a big football game. In this story, I included who attended and what they brought with them, as well as how much they ate and how much beer they consumed.

I chose some different arrays, variables, strings and numbers to explain the story throughout the timeline before the game started.

I did use quite a few functions as well as basic while and for loop statements.
